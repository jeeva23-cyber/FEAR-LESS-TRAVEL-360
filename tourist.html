Essential Travel Safety Guidelines

Quick checklist and protocols to ensure secure travel across tourist monument sites.

1. Before You Leave Hotel

• Keep your Digital Passport QR card downloaded offline on your device.
• Turn on your device's GPS Location settings and keep the dashboard open.
• Check active weather and warning panels on the Overview Dashboard widget.
• Set a reasonable geofence safe perimeter radius before leaving.

2. Safe Navigation Guidelines

• Stay within designated safe regions and boulevard monument zones.
• If you exit geofence boundaries, pay attention to the breach warnings.
• Do not travel down unlit paths or remote pathways after 8 PM.
• Ask the AI safety assistant for nearby verified police booth coordinates.

3. Emergency Siren Protocol

• If threat is imminent, click the prominent red SOS button immediately.
• Keep the browser window open; it plays a loud synthesized buzzer alarm.
• The system broadcasts coordinates to your designated contacts via SMS simulation.
• Alert logs are saved in the administration moderation desk for backup.

4. Security Verification & ID

• Present the QR Identity Card to monument check posts when requested.
• Medical emergency teams can scan the code to load blood group parameters.
• If you experience pickpocketing or harassment, file a media report immediately.
• Review the status of your reports in the admin dashboard panel.
