About FearLess 360

Learn about our mission, technology stack, and the Smart India Hackathon problem statements we address.

Our Mission

Deploying sub-second emergency response and safety monitoring platforms that empower domestic and international tourists to travel freely across Indian sites without security distress.

Our Vision

Enabling a highly secure, smart travel ecosystem in India by unifying local law enforcement units, hospitals, and dynamic GPS safety geofences within a single software dashboard.

Key Objectives

Create digital verified identity passes, build client-side geofencing zones, synthesize siren dispatch, and support incident logging tools optimized for mobile and desktop screens.

Smart India Hackathon Problem Statement

Title: AI-Powered Tourist Safety, Alerting, and Incident Response Framework.
Context: Tourists traveling to remote locations or crowded monuments frequently struggle to access local police help, emergency medical assets, or secure route warnings in real-time. Traditional safety apps suffer from high latency, complex forms, lack of location persistence, and offline display failure.

FearLess Travel 360 Solution: By combining responsive front-end frameworks with offline database backups (`localStorage`), client-side geofencing perimeters (`Leaflet`), and browser Audio Context sirens (`Web Audio`), we offer an immediate, low-latency safeguard interface that is completely suitable for SIH evaluations and deployable across government safety infrastructure.

Technology Infrastructure

A breakdown of premium developer frameworks and APIs unifying the safety components.

AI Engine (Mocked NLP)

Local parser engine resolving query intentions to provide instant contact lists and nearest safety guidelines.

Leaflet & OpenStreetMap

Bypasses paid Google Map API barriers to render smooth tile loaders, vector markers, and circle geofences.

QR Code Passport

Dynamic Canvas rendering script providing downloadable safety cards containing emergency data.

Local Storage

Client-side simulation of remote database, managing credentials profiles, incident logs, and SOS coordinates.

Blockchain ready

Data models pre-formatted into hashes, ready to lock digital user passports onto Ethereum blockchain nodes.

Chart.js Analytics

Admin data visualizer providing linear incident trends, count logs, and reported metrics charts.
